About me

Preacher | Teacher | Entreprenuer

Born and raised in Philadelphia, I married my college sweetheart and we've been together for 25 years with 3 awesome children. I'm a preacher, teacher and aspiring entrepreneur.

My favorite sport is football and I love spending time with my family and teaching bible study each week. I love to travel and experience different cultures.

I have been to several countries including, Senegal, The Gambia, France, South Korea, Burkina Faso, Cameroon, Israel and most recently to Malawi, Africa. These experiences have shaped how I view the world.

I'm currently enrolled in the Penn LPS Coding Bootcamp. I look forward to taking my interest in web development from part-time business to full-time freelance web development!
