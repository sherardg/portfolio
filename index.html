About me

Preacher | Teacher | Entreprenuer

Born and raised in Philadelphia, I married my college sweetheart and we have been together for 25 years and we have 3 children. I am a preacher, teacher and aspiring entrepreneur. My favorite sport is football and I love spending time with my family and teaching bible study each week. I love to travel and experience different cultures. I have been to several countries including, Senegal, The Gambia, France, South Korea, Burkina Faso, Cameroon, Malawi and Israel. These experiences have shaped how I view the world.

I am excited about this coding bootcamp and looking forward to taking my interest with web development from part-time to full-time freelance web development!
